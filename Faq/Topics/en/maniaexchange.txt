#Mania-Exchange, the ultimate map sharing site

$lhttp://www.mania-excange.com$l is a community driven map shareing website for ManiaPlanet games.
Uploading your map to the service, you enable it to be find by fellow players.
Your can grant and recieve awards for your maps also top time replay can be 
uploaded. And you can add your map to be played at MX PlayPal Online.

ManiaExchange has also a unique way to add very easily maps directly to dedicated servers,
